# Orderforge Foundation

## Brand
Orderforge

## Positioning
Practical tools and systems for organizing desks, workspaces, gaming setups, workshops, and small spaces.

## Audience
- Desk workers
- Gamers
- Makers
- Workshop tinkerers
- Small-space dwellers
- People trying to make their setup functional

## Brand qualities
- Clean
- Practical
- Modern
- Slightly premium
- Easy to trust

## Promise
Make clutter less stupid.

## Business model
### Phase 1
Sell digital products:
- PDFs
- editable templates
- labels
- planning packs

### Phase 2
Sell 3D-printed products:
- cable clips
- desk hooks
- controller/headphone holders
- drawer organizers
- tool holders
- pegboard accessories

### Phase 3
Sell STL files and mixed bundles.

## Why this niche
- Broad demand
- Low startup cost
- Can launch before printer arrives
- Natural bridge into physical products
- Less policy friction than restricted categories

## Pricing approach
Start low-to-mid priced to reduce friction:
- Entry products: $5–$9
- Bundles: $15–$19
- Physical products later: priced by utility, not novelty
