# Free-First Marketing System

## Rule
No paid ads until revenue exists.

## Traffic stack
### 1. Storefront SEO hygiene
- Clear product titles
- Buyer-language descriptions
- niche-specific offers
- bundles and upsells

### 2. Short-form content
Post practical, plain-language clips/images showing:
- what the pack includes
- before/after document upgrades
- examples of flyers, quote sheets, checklists
- "what small cleaning businesses actually need"

### 3. Direct outreach
Target local service operators directly:
- Facebook groups
- Instagram pages
- local Google Maps businesses
- community groups

### 4. Social proof assets
Create samples and mockups that look human and useful, not synthetic and cringe.

### 5. Offer ladder
- low-ticket template pack
- custom add-on
- later physical/3D item upsell

## Channels
### Priority
- Facebook groups / local groups
- Instagram outreach
- TikTok / Shorts
- Pinterest
- Reddit where relevant and non-spammy

## Content themes
- time saved
- cleaner presentation
- easier quoting / invoicing
- repeatable systems
- less admin chaos

## Messaging rule
Talk like an operator, not a guru.
Avoid hype. Lead with usefulness.

## First free-marketing campaign
### Product
House Cleaner Starter Pack

### Angle
"Look more organized, respond faster, and stop rebuilding the same quote/invoice/flyer every week."

### Assets needed
- 3 product mockups
- 5 captions
- 3 outreach messages
- 1 short landing-style summary

## Later paid ads
Only after:
- at least one product gets traction
- message is validated
- conversion angle is clearer
