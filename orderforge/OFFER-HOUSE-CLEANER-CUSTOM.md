# Custom Cleaner Starter Kit

## Offer
A done-for-you add-on for small residential cleaning businesses that want their materials customized instead of generic.

## Positioning
Fast customization for operators who need to look organized without hiring a designer.

## Core promise
Get a cleaner-looking business setup in 24 hours without wasting time assembling your own docs and promo materials.

## Suggested pricing
- Base custom add-on: $39
- Higher-touch version later: $79

## Deliverables
- Business name added
- Color palette adjusted
- Contact info inserted
- One flyer customized
- One quote template customized
- One invoice template customized
- Review link / QR code placement

## Fast delivery framing
Delivered in 24 hours after receiving:
- business name
- phone/email
- service area
- review link or website
- preferred colors (optional)

## Why this matters
This is the faster-cash lane. The digital packs build the catalog; custom work generates cash sooner and reveals what buyers actually need.

## Constraints
Keep scope brutally simple.
Do not promise logo design, websites, or endless revisions.

## Future expansion
- cleaner starter kit + print-ready flyer upsell
- cleaner starter kit + QR review stand preorder
- cleaner starter kit + branded label/tag preorder
