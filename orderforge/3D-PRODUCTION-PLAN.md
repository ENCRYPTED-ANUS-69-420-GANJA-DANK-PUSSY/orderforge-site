## Pricing Strategy

### Formula
(Material cost × 3) + (Print time × $0.50/hour) + Packaging + Platform fees = Base
Base × 2-3 = Retail price

### Example: QR Stand
- Filament: $0.50
- Time: 45min × $0.50 = $0.38
- Packaging: $1.00
- Platform fees: ~$1.50
- Base: $3.38
- Retail: $10-12
