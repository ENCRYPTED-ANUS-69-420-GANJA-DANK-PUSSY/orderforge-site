# Orderforge Pivot — 2026-04-02

## Decision
Orderforge will focus on practical business systems for solo local-service operators.

## First niche
House cleaners.

## Why
- already built
- already live
- clearer buyer pain than generic organization printables
- better zero-budget outreach path
- easier upsell into customization
- faster route to first revenue

## What changes now
- Stop positioning Orderforge as a mixed 3D/organization/cleaner brand.
- Treat 3D printing as a later separate lane after revenue and printer fluency exist.
- Use the site and content around the cleaner offer first.

## Offer ladder
1. House Cleaner Starter Pack — low-ticket
2. Custom Cleaner Starter Kit — higher-ticket upsell
3. Later: adjacent niches using the same system

## Immediate execution order
1. Rebuild landing page around cleaners first
2. Keep broader products as secondary or remove from homepage
3. Tighten outreach and posting system
4. Track responses and only expand after first sales data

## Human walls
- external logins
- captcha / verification
- publish clicks where auth is required
